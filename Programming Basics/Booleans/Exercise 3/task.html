Mastering the IDE: Navigating Back and Forward

You can easily navigate to a function declared in another file or to a function in a library. In this case, it's convenient to navigate backward afterward.

You navigate backward by pressing &shortcut:Back;, to navigate again forward press &shortcut:Forward;.

Alternatively, you may choose Navigate | Back in the application menu, and Navigate | Forward. You can also choose to navigate to Last Edit Location in the same menu.
