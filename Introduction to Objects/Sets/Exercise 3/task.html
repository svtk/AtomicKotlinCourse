Mastering the IDE: Duplicate / Delete Lines

It's easy to duplicate or delete a line:

OperationShortcutDescriptionDuplicate Line or Block&shortcut:EditorDuplicate;Duplicate selected block or the line at the caret.Delete Line at Caret&shortcut:EditorDeleteLine;Delete the line where the caret is currently located.

Display the list contents twice by duplicating the line in one click. Try duplicating a block of code; for instance, the contents of the list: 1, 2, 3, 4.
